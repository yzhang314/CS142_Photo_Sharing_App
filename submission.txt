Story number and name: 2 - @mention in comments
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action: 
You can mention a user in a comment by selecting from a menu of existing users. And you can go to each user profile to see which photo mentioned him/her. When you delete the comment, it will automatically delete the mention this comment contained shown in a user’s profile as well. Also if you mention a user in a photo that is not visible to him/her, it won’t show in his/her profile.

Story number and name: 3 - Visibility control on photos
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: 
You can control the visibility of a photo when you upload it. Click the button “upload” in the toolbar.
Here is a subtle difference as required. If you choose “do not specify visibility list”, the photo can only be seen by you. If you do not select any user , the photo can be seen by anyone.

Story number and name: 5 - Deleting Comments, Photos and Users
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: 
You can delete any comments, photos created by you. (There will be delete buttons only shown for every one of “your” photos and comments, just click it and you can delete) You can also delete your account in your profile page by clicking the button.

Story number and name: 6 - Photo like votes
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: 
You can like a photo by clicking the like button besides it. There will be a number shown how many users like it and also an indication that you liked it.

Story number and name: 7 - Favorite lists of photos for users
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action: 
You can favorite a photo by clicking the favorite button besides it. There will be an indication shown that you have favorited it. Go to your favorite list (button in the toolbar) to see the modal or delete any photos from your favorite list. (You can also do it by unfavorite).
